"A blockchain project: A decentralized carbon credit marketplace where users can mint, trade, and retire carbon offset credits as NFTs. Environmental organizations and carbon offset projects can mint verified carbon credits as unique tokens, while individuals and businesses can purchase these credits to offset their carbon footprint. The smart contract includes functionality for credit verification, automatic retirement tracking, and transparent reporting of offset activities. Users earn reputation scores based on their offset history, and the platform includes a simple staking mechanism where STX holders can stake tokens to vote on which carbon projects get featured or verified."
